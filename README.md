# [Magento 2 Size Chart Extension](https://magefan.com/magento-2-size-chart) by Magefan

<img align="right" width="120" height="120" src="https://cm.magefan.com/mf_webp/jpg/media/catalog/product/cache/a1683bce6f623be8eb25d859e5ca6d6f/i/c/icon-size-chart.webp">

[Magento 2 Size Chart Extension](https://magefan.com/magento-2-size-chart)  is a solution that allows merchnats to create attrative size charts in a few clicks with not coding. Use ready-made template for the most popular categories or create your size charts from scratch.

Display personalzied size charts on separate products or entire categories. Place size charts where people can find them - in popups or product tabs. 

Reduce returns and help customers choose the right size the first time. 

Compatibility: Magento 2.3.0 - 2.4.x (CE, EE, ECE, B2B)

## Features
### Reduce Returns and Make Shopping in Your Store Simple

Online shopping shouldn’t feel like a challenge. Yet, for many customers, choosing the right size feels overwhelming. This often leads to abandoned carts or returns that cost you money.

Reduce sizing uncertainty with the Magento 2 Size Chart Extension.

Help customers choose the perfect fit with easy-to-understand size charts tied to different products.

### Use Done-for-you Templates — No Setup Stress
Creating size charts from scratch is a lost game. You have to find the accurate measurements, explore different sizes and internalional standard. That’s why we’ve done the hard part for you.

Start fast with 4 ready-made templates for the most common clothing categories, including:

- Women’s Tops & Bottoms
- Men’s Tops & Bottoms

Just pick a template, make changes, and post.

<p align="center">
  <img width="853" height="425" src="https://cm.magefan.com/mf_webp/png/media/wysiwyg/products/size-chart-templates-magento.webp">
</p>

### Fully Customisable to Match Your Brand
Except for being unique to different products, size charts should reflect your unique style. 

Magento 2 Size Chart Extension ensures that with easily customisable charts. Add your brand colours, fonts, icons, and visuals to create brandable size charts.

Create a seamless shopping experience that looks and feels like your brand — even in the smallest details.

<p align="center">
  <img width="853" height="425" src="https://cm.magefan.com/mf_webp/png/media/wysiwyg/products/customize-size-charts-magento.webp">
</p>

### Create Smart Display Rules
Different products need different charts. Show customers exactly what they need automatically.

Use built-in dispay conditions to automate size chart visibility based on:

- Product type or category
- Customer group or location
- Any custom rule you define

Serve the right chart at the right time regardlss of the product type.

<p align="center">
  <img width="853" height="425" src="https://cm.magefan.com/mf_webp/png/media/wysiwyg/products/size-charts-conditions-magento.webp">
</p>

### Show It Where It Matters Most
If customers can’t find your size chart, it won’t help them. That’s why our Magento 2 Size Chart extension gives you flexibility over the size chart position. 

Choose to display your chart inside product tabs or popups.You have full control over tab position and label attributes. 

Make sure your size charts are seen. Make sure they help. Every time.
